Frequently Asked Questions

Do you do sneaker authentication?

While I have experience with authentication, I recommend using professional services like StockX, GOAT, or CheckCheck for official authentication. I'm happy to share general tips and red flags to watch for.

Can you help me start streaming?

Absolutely! I love helping newcomers get started. Feel free to reach out with specific questions about equipment, software, or building your first community. I remember how overwhelming it can be at first.

What's a good entry point into horror?

Check out my recommendations section on the horror page! I've curated lists specifically for beginners. Start with something like "A Quiet Place" or "The Others" before diving into the more intense stuff.

Do you collaborate with brands?

I'm selective about partnerships and only work with brands that align with my values and genuinely benefit my community. If you're interested in collaboration, please include details about your brand and proposal.

Can I suggest content ideas?

Yes! I love hearing from the community about what they'd like to see. Whether it's a specific sneaker review, horror game playthrough, or streaming topic, your suggestions help shape my content.

How did you get into these hobbies?

Each interest developed naturally over time - check out my About page for the full story! I'm always happy to share more details about my journey and what keeps me passionate about these areas.
